Download Cinch Audio Recorder

📌 Overview

Cinch Audio Recorder helps you save music from streaming services to your computer. The software captures audio directly from your sound card. This method keeps the original sound quality. You can save songs as MP3 files. This tool works on Windows 11 and Windows 10.

  • Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11
  • Processor: 1 GHz or faster
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Storage: 200 MB of free space
  • Internet connection for streaming access

⚙️ Using the Software

Recording audio happens in three steps:

  1. Open the Cinch Audio Recorder application from your desktop shortcut.
  2. Click the "Record" button inside the app interface.
  3. Play your music in your preferred streaming application or browser.
  4. The software automatically separates tracks and adds ID3 tags like artist name and song title.
  5. Click the "Stop" button when you finish listening.

💡 Tips for Best Results

Keep your system volume at a normal level while recording. The software records the audio signal from your system. Do not mute your computer speakers while the recording runs. Muting ends the audio signal and stops the software from capturing the sound. Close other programs that play system sounds, such as notifications or alarms, to ensure your music files remain clean. Use wired headphones or speakers to verify the quality of your output files after recording.
